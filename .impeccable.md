## Design Context

### Users
Language learners (beginner-intermediate) exploring Turkish through an immersive city RPG. Playing on mobile phones, likely during downtime — commute, evening on the couch. The session is 5 minutes.

### Brand Personality
Mysterious, playful, adventurous

### Aesthetic Direction
Indie adventure game. Think Monument Valley's clean geometry and atmospheric color with Duolingo's rewarding game mechanics. Dark theme justified by the "sunset in Istanbul" narrative and evening phone usage. Warm amber as the accent — derived from Istanbul's actual golden-hour light. NOT a flashcard app. NOT a corporate learning platform. This is a pocket-sized adventure.

### Design Principles
1. **Every screen is a scene** — the player should feel like they're IN Istanbul, not looking at a UI
2. **Words are collectibles** — Turkish vocabulary should feel like treasure discovered, not homework assigned
3. **Reward generously** — correct answers, mystery reveals, and level completion should all feel earned and celebrated
4. **Sound and sight together** — the dual-channel (audio + visual) is the core mechanic, never break it
5. **Simple surfaces, rich depth** — clean UI with atmospheric layering underneath (illustrations, gradients, audio)
